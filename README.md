# symmusic.github.io
Providing Internet music access to older and newer Symbian phones through simplified apps that can still be accessed through mobile browsers.

## Description
The symmusic.github.io project aims to deliver a seamless music streaming experience to users of Symbian phones, both old and new. By leveraging lightweight and efficient web applications, users can enjoy their favorite music directly from their mobile browsers without the need for complex installations or heavy applications. This project ensures that even legacy devices can access modern music streaming services with ease.

## Features
- **Compatibility**: Works with both older and newer Symbian phones.
- **Lightweight**: Designed to be efficient and fast, even on legacy hardware.
- **Browser-Based**: No need for complex installations; accessible through mobile browsers.
- **User-Friendly**: Simplified interface for easy navigation and use.
- **Extensive Library**: Access a wide range of music genres and artists.
- **Regular Updates**: Continuously updated to improve performance and add new features.

## Getting Started
1. Open your mobile browser on your Symbian phone.
2. Navigate to [symmusic.github.io](https://anyosil.github.io/symmusic.github.io).
3. Start streaming your favorite music instantly.

## Usage
1. **Search**: Use the search bar to find your favorite songs or artists.
2. **Play**: Click on a song's play button to start playing it.
3. **Share**: Share your favorite music with friends via social media or direct links.
